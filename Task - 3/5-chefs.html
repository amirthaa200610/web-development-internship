Meet the Culinary Masters

Arjun Mehta

At the helm of our kitchen is Chef Arjun Mehta, an award-winning culinary artist with over 15 years of experience. Trained at Le Cordon Bleu, Paris, he has led kitchens in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe and Asia. Known for his expertise in global fusion, Chef Arjun has been featured in Culinary Vogue India and was awarded "Innovative Chef of the Year" in 2023.

"Cooking isn’t just about taste — it’s about telling a story on every plate."

Meera Nair

A dynamic force in our kitchen, Chef Meera Nair brings creativity and finesse to every dish she touches. With a background in modern European and Indian fusion cuisine, she blends flavors with an artistic touch. Meera is the genius behind our Rasam Malai Tiramisu, a bold dessert that reimagines tradition with elegance and flair.

Rohan Kapoor

Supporting our kitchen’s creative vision is Chef Rohan Kapoor, a passionate and detail-driven sous chef with over 7 years of experience. Trained in contemporary Indian and Mediterranean cuisines, Rohan brings precision, consistency, and innovation to every service. He is the creative mind behind our unique Chicken Tikka Lasagna, a dish that perfectly blends bold Indian flavors with comforting Italian layers.

Ananya Rao

With a sharp palate and a love for global flavors, Chef Ananya Rao brings balance and innovation to the table. Specializing in plant-forward and fusion concepts, she crafts dishes that are both vibrant and soulful. Her signature creation, the Falafel Nachos, is a bold twist on comfort food that has quickly become a guest favorite.
